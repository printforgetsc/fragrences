Our Philosophy

Where Science
Meets Sorcery

We believe a great perfume is a form of art that transcends the senses. Our master perfumers — trained in Grasse, refined by decades of practice — compose each fragrance as a symphonic narrative.

From the first breath to the lingering drydown, every LUMIÈRE creation tells a complete story. We use only naturals of the highest provenance, alongside a carefully chosen palette of captive molecules unavailable to anyone else.

Provenance

Every ingredient is traced to its origin — Bulgarian rose fields, Mysore sandalwood forests, Madagascan vanilla vines — with direct relationships that ensure ethical harvest and uncompromising quality.

02

Alchemy

Our atelier employs both ancient maceration techniques and contemporary molecular distillation, allowing us to capture aromatic facets that traditional methods simply cannot reach.

03

Longevity

We formulate at 25–35% concentration — far exceeding industry norms — ensuring that each application evolves beautifully for twelve hours or more on the skin.

Signature Creation

Oud Impérial
Nocturne

Eau de Parfum · 50ml & 100ml

A commanding oriental fragrance built around a rare Laos oud accord. Opening with cardamom and saffron, it blooms into a heart of Turkish rose and labdanum, settling into an extraordinary base of aged oud, ambergris, and smoked sandalwood. An experience reserved for those who seek the exceptional.
